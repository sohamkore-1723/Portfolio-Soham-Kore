As a second-year student at Atlas SkillTech University, I am deeply immersed in the world of communication design, where I aspire to blend creativity with strategic thinking. My journey as an aspiring designer has been fueled by a passion for graphics and branding, allowing me to explore the nuances of visual storytelling. Join me on this creative journey as I strive to make an impact through design.
